04 / StudioEst. 2019 — Three continents

We are a small studio with an outsized appetite for the impossible.

Founded in Lisbon in 2019, Prismatic grew from a two-person experiment in real-time graphics into a twenty-two person collective spanning three cities and every timezone that matters.

We are not an agency. We are not a production house. We are a spatial practice — a group of engineers, directors, and material scientists who believe the browser is the most powerful canvas ever invented.

Every project begins with a single question: what would it look and feel like if this were a place, not just a page?
